Petsbook rules

Every day, pets come to Petsbook to share their stories, see the world through the eyes of others, and connect with friends and causes.

We recognize how important it is for Petsbook to be a place where pets feel empowered to communicate, and we take our role in keeping abuse off our service seriously. That’s why we have developed a set of Community Standards that outline what is and is not allowed on Petsbook.

Our Standards apply around the world to all types of content. They’re designed to be comprehensive – for example, content that might not be considered hate speech may still be removed for violating our bullying policies.

    The main policies include following:
  • No bark policy
  • No catfish policy
  • No bullying policy
  • Don't look a horse in the mouth
  • Let the cat meow
  • No wild goose chase
  • Don't be mad as a hornet

Everyone who will break the rule will be banned for eternity. Be a good boy.
