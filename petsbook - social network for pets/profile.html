One cat in a box

Published: Aug 7, 2018

Have you ever thought why we love boxes so much? Recently I discovered why!

Seeking out confined spaces is an instinctual behavior for cats. In the wild, these areas allow us to both hide from predators and surreptitiously stalk prey. ... Boxes also provide cats with a cozy, safe place to sleep, which is very important given that the felines sleep a lot.

So go grab your favorite box and enjoy your wilderness :)
